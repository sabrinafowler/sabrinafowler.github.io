I am a PhD candidate in my fourth year at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am advised by Eloísa Grifo. For the Spring 2025 semester, I am an RTG Trainee with Commutative Algebra at Nebraska. I am interested in symbolic powers of ideals, Gröbner bases and sheaf theory. I am also interested in using these techniques to solve problems in topological data analysis and mathematical neuroscience. My email is sfowler14@unl.edu and my office is Avery Hall 232.
